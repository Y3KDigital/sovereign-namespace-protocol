## 6) Telnyx readiness checklist (operator-grade)

This section is a non-implementation plan you can hand to a partner.

- **Port/hosting:** ensure you can demonstrate you control numbers and can re-point routing fast.
- **Webhook verification:** require cryptographic verification for inbound Telnyx webhooks (prevents spoofed event injection).
- **STIR/SHAKEN + CNAM:** maximize answer rates and reduce spam labeling.
- **Consent + recording policy:** store consent events with call session IDs.
- **Routing logs:** store call session identifiers + outcomes (answered, escalated, abandoned).
- **Disaster mode:** ability to route all calls to a human concierge or voicemail capture if AI provider degraded.
