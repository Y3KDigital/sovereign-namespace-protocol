## Why This Phased Approach?

### Protocol First, Infrastructure Second

**This is intentional, not a limitation.**

**Comparable Launches:**
- **Bitcoin (2009)**: Protocol launched, wallets came later, exchanges much later
- **Ethereum (2015)**: Blockchain launched, developer tools built over years
- **ENS (2017)**: Registry launched, integrations added progressively

**Y3K follows the same model:**
1. **Launch**: Genesis ceremony + root issuance (January 2026)
2. **Build**: Brand infrastructure + verification (Q1-Q2 2026)
3. **Integrate**: Ecosystem adapters + partnerships (Q2-Q3 2026)

---

### Why Not Wait Until "Everything Is Ready"?

**Three reasons:**

**1. Genesis Ceremony is One-Time Event**
- Cannot be repeated
- January 16, 2026 is the creation moment
- Delay = missed genesis date

**2. Root Scarcity is Immediate**
- 1,000 roots sell out once, never return
- Early adopters get best positions (#1-100)
- Value accrues to early participants

**3. Protocol Maturity Requires Real Use**
- Developer feedback shapes infrastructure
- Brand pilot informs feature priorities
- Early adopters guide roadmap
